🛡️ Social Protection

Non-take-up Reduction

BELGIUM / FRANCE

Algorithms cross-reference income and housing databases to identify citizens entitled to benefits who haven't applied, automating data collection to pre-fill applications.

Magic Notes (OECD 2025)

UNITED KINGDOM

AI transcribes and summarizes social workers' home visits. This ensures critical risk signals aren't lost in paperwork while allowing more human-centric care.

Intelligent Fraud Detection

SPAIN / BRAZIL

ML models identify atypical claim patterns, drastically reducing "false positives" compared to older systems, protecting innocent beneficiaries.

Custom Rehab Plans

SOUTH KOREA

AI analyzes millions of historical records of workplace accidents to suggest the rehabilitation plan with the highest probability of success for a specific worker.

Eviction Prediction

USA / EUROPE

Predictive models identify households at risk of homelessness via utility payment delays, triggering proactive financial aid before eviction occurs.

🏥 Healthcare

Diagnostic Imaging

SINGAPORE / SA

AI analyzes X-rays and mammograms to detect anomalies (TB, cancer) with high precision, prioritizing urgent cases for immediate physician review.

Ambient Dictation

GLOBAL (WHO)

AI tools listen to consultations and generate structured clinical notes automatically, allowing doctors to focus on the patient instead of typing.

Resource Management

OECD NETWORK

AI predicts patient admission peaks (flu/heatwaves), optimizing bed usage, staff allocation, and medication inventory dynamically.
